🧠 ResearchMindAI

AI-Powered Research Assistant for Scientific Literature Analysis

ResearchMindAI is a full-stack AI research platform that helps researchers, students, and academics analyze scientific papers using Generative AI, Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), semantic search, and automated research intelligence workflows.

🚀 Features
📄 Research Paper Upload
Upload PDF research papers
Automatic text extraction
Metadata extraction
Intelligent chunking
Embedding generation
🤖 Ask Paper
Ask natural language questions about uploaded papers
Context-aware answers using RAG
Semantic retrieval of relevant sections
📚 Literature Review Generator
Automatically generates structured literature reviews
Synthesizes findings across uploaded papers
Highlights methodologies and trends
🎯 Research Gap Analysis
Identifies existing challenges
Detects limitations of current approaches
Finds unexplored opportunities
Suggests future research directions
Generates innovative project ideas
📄 Research Proposal Generator

Creates complete research proposals including:

Title
Abstract
Problem Statement
Literature Review
Research Gap
Objectives
Methodology
Expected Results
Future Scope
References
⚖️ Multi-Paper Comparison
Compare multiple research papers
Analyze methodologies
Compare results and performance
Identify strengths and weaknesses
Recommend best approaches
📊 Research Score

Evaluates research ideas based on:

Novelty
Technical Depth
Feasibility
Impact
Publication Potential
📝 IEEE Reviewer Mode

Simulates an IEEE reviewer by generating:

Strengths
Weaknesses
Novelty Assessment
Technical Quality Review
Publication Recommendation
